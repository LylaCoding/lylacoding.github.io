Deciding what's right for you

This guide will go over deciding which type of HRT works best for you, covering briefly what each type is, how it is applied, and wether it can be used for monotherapy.
Unfortunately, going into costs on this page would not work as prices and vendors change all the time. Check out diyhrt.market for up to date prices

Injections
Description: Injections are one of the most efficient form of HRT. It involes using a needle to inject yourself subcutaneously or intramuscular.

Application: Subcutaneously or intramuscular injection into either the lower abdomen or thighs. Usually done on a scheduled basis (For example Every week/fortnight/month).

Can be used for monotherapy?:
Estrogen: Yes
Testosterone: Yes

Sprays
Description: Sprays are applied transdermally and can be applied in multiple places of the body. Due to bottles usually being small, you may need to purchase in either bulk or more often.

Application: Applied transdermally to either shoulders, arms (avoid inner elbow), armpit, or thighs. Would need to be applied mulitiple times a day.

Can be used for monotherapy?:
Estrogen: Yes
Testosterone: Yes

Gels
Description: Gels are applied transdermally and can be applied in multiple places of the body. Due to bottles usually being small, you may need to purchase in either bulk or more often.

Application: Applied transdermally to either shoulders, arms (avoid inner elbow), armpit, or thighs. Would need to be applied mulitiple times a day.

Can be used for monotherapy?:
Estrogen: No
Testosterone: Yes

Pills
Description: Pretty much the worst kind if HRT, costs are quite high and not effective. It is never recommended to take Testosterone pills. Estrogen pills should also be taken with an anti-androgen (like very low dose cypro, never do spiro).

Application: Estrogen pills should be taken sublingually or buccally. Taking Estrogen pills orally (swalling them) severely decreases their effects. Pills need to be taken at multiple intervals in a day. (For example taking a pill every 6-8 hours)

Can be used for monotherapy?:
Estrogen: Not at all
Testosterone: Don't even try
